Finishing nails are commonly used to carefully fasten small wood pieces to walls and cabinetry. They are made as small as possible in order to damage the wood as little as possible.
What pressure in Pascals is created on a finishing nail with a diameter of ${{params.d}} mm$ if it is hit by a hammer with a force of ${{params.F}} N$?
