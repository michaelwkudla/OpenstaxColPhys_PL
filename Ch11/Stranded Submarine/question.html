A submarine is stranded on the bottom of the ocean with its hatch {{params.depth}} $m$ below the surface. Air pressure inside the submarine is $1.00 atm$.
Part 1
What is the relative pressure outside the hatch?
Part 2
Calculate the force needed to open the hatch from the inside, given it is circular and {{params.d}} $m$ in diameter.
