The Sodastream is a device which consumers can use to create their own soda water from regular tap water. A pressurized bottle is used to push $CO_2$ from the bottle into the users water. Assume that the Sodastream is originally at a Pressure of ${{params.P1}} kPa$ before filling some bottles, and ${{params.P2}} kPa$ after filling. If the temperature of the bottle was originally at ${{params.T1}} ^{\circ}C$, what is the final temperature of the bottle?
