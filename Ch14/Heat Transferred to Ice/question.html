We wish to determine how much heat transfer is necessary to raise the temperature of a ${{params.m}} kg$ piece of ice from ${{params.T1}} ^{\circ} C$ to ${{params.T2}} ^{\circ} C$, including the energy needed for phase changes.
Part 1
How much heat is transferred from ${{params.T1}} ^{\circ} C$ to $0 ^{\circ} C$?
Part 2
How much heat is transferred to melt the ice?
Part 3
How much heat is transferred from $0 ^{\circ} C$ to $100 ^{\circ} C$?
Part 4
How much heat is transferred to boil the water?
Part 5
How much heat is transferred from $100 ^{\circ} C$ to ${{params.T2}} ^{\circ} C$?
Part 6
What is the total energy transferred?
