There are many entertaining videos online of people jumping off their roofs or ledges onto a trampoline. It rarely ends well. A trampoline of height ${{params.h1}} m$ has an overall spring force constant of ${{params.k}}$ $N/m$ in the middle of the trampoline.
Part 1
A girl with mass ${{params.m}} kg$ jumps from a deck of height ${{params.h2}} m$ onto the middle of the trampoline. What is the maximum displacement of thhe trampoline when she is at the bottom of her bounce? Ignore the energy gained by the girl as the trampoline is stretched.
