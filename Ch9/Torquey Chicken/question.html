Foghorn Leghorn the chicken is standing on one foot, leaning into the wind. The chickens mass is {{params.m}}, $kg$, and is centered {{params.d2}} $m$ away from the foot he is standing on. The force of the wind can be assumed to be applied horizontally a distance of {{params.d1}} $m$ off the ground.
Part 1
What force must be exerted by the wind to support Foghorn Leghorn?
Part 2
What is the ratio of this force to the chickens weight?
